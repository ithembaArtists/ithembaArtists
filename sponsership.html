SPONSORSHIP

How can I get involved at iThemba School Capricorn?

To achieve our project goals, iThemba School Capricorn relies on the generosity of others. Beside the classical way of donating to iThemba there are many other ways you can support our project. For example, you can do voluntary work for iThemba or you can become a sponsor.

We are looking forward to jointly bettering the lives of 75 children of the township Capricorn by providing good education and a stable social environment.

Please join us in making it happen for young and eager children in the Vrygrond community. It could be the best investment you can make during these uncertain times. Thank you for your contribution.

Warmest regards,
Directors of iThemba School Capricorn

Take over social responsibility as a company

Impoverished children are the weakest members of our society, and they depend on your support and involvement. 

The importance of voluntary social engagement of companies has increased dramatically in recent years. Companies recognise more and more that responsible actions lead to long-lasting company success. iThemba School Capricorn offers your company a variety of options to engage with us for the future of these children.


Donation and Sponsorship

 

Sponsorship is the traditional way to support iThemba School Capricorn. If you want to build up a long-term relationship between your company and the iThemba School Capricorn, then this is the right option for you!
We distinguish between sponsorships that support individual children and those that support the entire school. All sponsors receive regular information and updates on activities and improvements at the school.


Donations as a gift on behalf of your clients

Is an important client of yours celebrating an important milestone? Do you want to reciprocate to a client?
Give a long lasting humanitarian gift instead of a material one. A donation "in wrapping paper" means: Donate in the name of your partner or client as a gesture of solidarity and humanity.


Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We can assist companies and decision makers to reach their social responsibilities by integrating the iThemba School Capricorn into their CSR strategy. Together with you, we tailor-make a partnership model that fits you individually and helps you realise even more through this arrangement.


Help more pre-schoolers to get a head start to their future!

Donations are all used towards the running costs of the school and we are also able to issue tax exemptions for your donations.

If there are certain areas you would like your donation to be used for, please let us know when making a donation.

MY SCHOOL


What is the MySchool programme?

As you might already know, we at iThemba have decided to
make iThemba School Capricorn a part of the MySchool Community.

MySchool is South Africa's foremost community support programme, contributing more than R1.5 million to schools and charities every month.  To date, the programme has raised over R63 million. MySchool supports South African learners by creating a sustainable income for schools through a collaborative community support model. The programme gives parents and other community-minded individuals a vehicle through which to make a meaningful contribution to schools, in a convenient and transparent manner. For more information visit their website: www.myschool.co.za.

The best part? It doesn't cost you, as a supporter, anything to make a difference!

What can you do to support the MySchool programme?

The MySchool card is not a debit or credit card. In fact, it does not cost you a cent. The card allows you to raise valuable funds for your chosen school or charity every time you swipe it at over 2,500 participating MySchool Business Partners (these include Woolworths, Engen, Waltons, Toys ‘R' Us, Reggie's, Quicksilver, Supaquick, Kalahari.net and many many more). Apply online and select "iThemba Pre-School Capricorn" as your beneficiary.

If you already have a MySchool card and would like to change the beneficiary to iThemba Pre-School Capricorn, please phone the Client Service Centre on Tel 0860 100 445 (you need to have your ID no. or MySchool card number ready).

It is very important that all fields are completed when you submit these forms to the MySchool programme. Your attention and effort are much appreciated and will go a long way in benefiting our school and ensuring that contributions are channeled and managed correctly. Apply for the card, and once you receive it start shopping and swiping at any MySchool Business Partner. Every swipe will raise funds for our school.

VOLUNTEER WORK?

Participate, Organise, Help, Donate

Work voluntarily at iThemba School Capricorn and personally help create a secure future for the children of the Capricorn township.

As a voluntary associate you can decide what skills or services you want to contribute. For example, you can organise a donation run, a street festival or an information stand whereby you can inform more people about our project. That way, you can actively contribute to achieving the goals of iThemba School Capricorn and help to ensure a first-class education for these children.

With your experience we can create a strong team together! Of course, we can also send you information material free of charge for your event, for interested friends or other supporters of iThemba School Capricorn.

About us

iThemba School Capricorn is a pre-primary school for 75 children between the age of 3 and 6 years old. Any contributions will help provide a safe and creative learning enviroment where children can flourish and develop to their full potential.
